About

Welcome to my portfolio!

I am a Virginia based game developer, with an unwavering passion for solving game development challenges. I currently work as a Unity developer for SAIC and have experience developing webGL 3D games including simulations, as well as mobile and web applications, VR, and SCORM compliant courseware for Learning Management Systems.

Additionally, I am also an ardent musician, which further fuels my creative drive.

I extend to you a heartfelt invitation to embark on a journey through my portfolio, where you will discover a captivating array of projects that showcase my versatility and artistic vision. If you have any questions about work that I have done or if you would like to see additional projects, feel free to shoot me a message!
