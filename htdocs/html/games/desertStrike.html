Desert Strike

Desert Strike is an adrenaline-fueled on-rails 3D first-person shooter that thrusts players into a dangerous training ground teeming with simulated real-world targets. Players must demonstrate their precision and speed with each passing moment. The intensity pushes players to their limits as they compete for the highest score. The unique on-rails gameplay mechanics keep the action fast-paced and immersive, propelling players through a visually stunning desert landscape. With its heart-pounding challenges and addictive gameplay, Desert Strike offers an unforgettable experience that will keep players engaged and striving for supremacy on the leader boards.
