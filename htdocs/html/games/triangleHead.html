In the distant future, the galaxy faces a grave threat from invasive drones that are wreaking havoc on planets, leaving destruction in their wake. These drones are controlled from a mysterious off-world planet, where a malevolent force seeks to conquer the entire galaxy. In response to this peril, a courageous group known as the Armored Crusade emerges to defend the galaxy and restore peace! Inspired by the TOMYTRONIC virtual reality game SKY ATTACK!.
