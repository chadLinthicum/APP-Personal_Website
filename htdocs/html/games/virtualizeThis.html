Chad Linthicum
Armored Crusade is an ambitious 3D action tank simulator with coin-op arcade gameplay! This game served as a prototype for a third game that will be introduced to the I.T.E. platform in 2024. Check out the Adobe XD wireframe I created for the pitch: HERE In the distant future, the galaxy faces a grave threat from invasive drones that are wreaking havoc on planets, leaving destruction in their wake. These drones are controlled from a mysterious off-world planet, where a malevolent force seeks to conquer the entire galaxy. In response to this peril, a courageous group known as the Armored Crusade emerges to defend the galaxy and restore peace! Based on the TOMYTRONIC game Sky Attack.
