Clean Sweep

Clean Sweep is an exhilarating first-person on-rails shooter that thrusts players into the heart-pounding action of navigating down a treacherous river on a military boat. Armed with precision and speed, players must utilize their shooting skills to take down an onslaught of challenging targets in the form of barrels. The objective is clear: achieve the highest score possible by obliterating as many barrels as possible within the given time limit. As players skillfully maneuver through the rushing waters, they are faced with ever-increasing obstacles and challenging environmental conditions that put their accuracy and reflexes to the ultimate test. With its immersive gameplay, realistic graphics, and adrenaline-pumping challenges, Clean Sweep delivers an experience that will keep players on the edge of their seats as they strive to dominate the leader boards and become the ultimate sharpshooter.
